ABOUT
US

We are researchers that will
build a website dedicated to a persons budgeting tool
tracking Weekly Expenses and Enhancing Savings Management
in a week.

It aims to develop a simple and effective personal budgeting tool
that helps users track their weekly expenses and find ways to save money.
Many people struggle to manage their finances and stick to a budget.
This tool will allow users to input their income and expenses, and track their spending.
The goal is to create an easy-to-use tool that helps users understand their finances better
and make smarter decisions to reach their savings goals.

This project was done by Romanillos et. al.
